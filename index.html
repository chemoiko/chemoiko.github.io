About Us

At kollody Agro Enterprise, we are passionate about agriculture and believe in the power of value addition. With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in adding value to farm products such as fertilizer, insecticide, and pesticides. We also engage in buying and selling a variety of essential crops including rice, nuts, beans, floor, and maize. Additionally, we provide top-notch farming equipment, set up efficient irrigation systems, and conduct sensitization programs to promote best farming practices.

Our Products

Fertilizers

Boost your crop productivity with our premium quality fertilizers tailored to meet the specific nutrient needs of your crops.

Insecticides & Pesticides

Our products are designed to control and eliminate a variety of insects ensuring the health and productivity of your plants.

Crop Seeds

All types of seeds that are resistant to drought and other climate conditions and Crop protection.

Farming Equipment and Tools

Enhance your farming operations with our top-of-the-line equipment and tools. From modern machinery to essential farming implements, we offer reliable products to streamline your agricultural activities.

Our Services

Farm Product Value Addition

Take your farm produce to the next level with our value addition solutions. We provide innovative techniques and technologies to help you transform your raw produce into high-demand products, maximizing profitability.
