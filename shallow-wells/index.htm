Shallow Well Solutions

At I.B.O Water Engineering Solutions, we specialize in providing expert shallow well digging services tailored to meet your water needs. Our team of experienced professionals utilizes advanced drilling techniques and high-quality equipment to ensure efficient and reliable water supply for various applications. Whether you require a shallow well for domestic use, agricultural irrigation, or commercial purposes, we have the expertise to deliver exceptional results.

Well water can be used for;

1.Irrigation

2.Domestic Use

3. Industrial Use

4. Commercial Use

Steps involved in constructing hand-dug water wells in Uganda

Step 1 – Survey

A physical visit is done to determine the probability at which a shallow dug well can be successful.

 We conduct a thorough site assessment to determine the best location for your shallow well, considering factors such as soil composition, water table depth, and proximity to existing structures.

Step 2 - Digging


The next step is to organize the digging team, tools, and materials to use. Tools include; pickaxe, spade, rope, bucket, pulley system to mention

Once water is reached, building materials are organized and delivered to the site. Building materials include; bricks (clay burnt bricks recommended), Sand (Lake sand recommended), cement, waterproof powder to mention.


Step 3 - Casing

We install durable well casings to protect the well from contamination and ensure long-term performance

Step 4 - Pump Installation

Upon completion of the well, we install high quality submersible pumps to efficiently extract water for your specific needs.
